Meet Grumpy: Colchester Zoo’s Moodiest Meerkat Baby

Colchester Zoo’s newborn meerkat, Grumpy, has stolen hearts with its adorably grouchy expression since its arrival on January 1, 2024.

Colchester Zoo is buzzing with excitement following the arrival of its newest resident—a tiny meerkat pup affectionately nicknamed Grumpy. Born on January 1, 2025, this adorable addition has already made a name for itself thanks to its uniquely expressive face, which earned it the nickname. Staff couldn’t help but chuckle at the pup’s seemingly disapproving look, a feature that has quickly endeared it to visitors and keepers alike.

Grumpy is part of the zoo's thriving meerkat family, which continues to captivate guests with their playful antics and close-knit dynamics. The pup is the latest offspring of the zoo’s dominant pair, who have successfully raised several litters over the years. At just a few days old, Grumpy is still dependent on its mother but has been spotted nestled safely in the burrow under the watchful eyes of its family.

“Grumpy’s face gave us a good laugh when it was born—it’s rare to see such a tiny meerkat look so serious!”

Emily, Zoo Keeper at Colchester Zoo

Zoo staff are monitoring Grumpy and the rest of the litter closely to ensure their development stays on track. Meerkat pups are born blind and helpless, relying entirely on their mother and the support of their group for survival. In just a few weeks, visitors can expect to see the youngster venturing out of the burrow and beginning to explore its surroundings.

“The pup is thriving under the care of its family, and we can’t wait to see its personality shine as it starts exploring the world outside the burrow.”

Emily, Zoo Keeper at Colchester Zoo

As the youngest member of the meerkat mob, Grumpy’s arrival has brought a festive spark to the end of 2024. Visitors to Colchester Zoo during the holiday season are already hoping to catch a glimpse of the little pup and its adorably grouchy expression. Though small now, it won’t be long before Grumpy is dashing about, joining its siblings in their lively routines.

The zoo hopes Grumpy will help raise awareness about meerkats and the challenges they face in the wild. Native to the arid regions of southern Africa, meerkats are known for their incredible teamwork and adaptability. Grumpy’s story serves as a reminder of the importance of conserving wildlife and celebrating the unique quirks of every animal, big or small.
