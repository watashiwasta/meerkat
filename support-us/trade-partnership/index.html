Trade Partnership

Become a Trade Partner and receive a 'special buying rate' for tickets to visit Colchester Zoo.

View 2024 Trade Rate and Contract Corporate Sponsorships

All Trade Partners have a ‘special buying rate’ from Colchester Zoo. The saving that Trade Partners make can be passed onto their customers or alternatively used as an opportunity to generate commission or fundraise for a charity. Trade Partners may sell tickets at any price between the trade rate and the full day admission price available at Colchester Zoo.
